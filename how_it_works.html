How it works:

Our app transforms the way you save by categorizing your funds into “Cookie Jars”, turning aspirations into achievable milestones. Gain crystal-clear visibility into your finances while building a smarter, more empowered financial future. But we don't stop there—stay ahead with cutting-edge financial trends and exclusive insights from industry-leading experts, all delivered right to your fingertips.

Smart budgeting made easy:
  • Split your total monthly income into savings, expenses and other goals so you are never caught off-guard by sudden emergency expenses.
  • Divide the remaining income into more Cookie Jars, and regularly review and adjust your budget.
  • Personalise the ’budget tracker’ to fit your needs and saving styles.
  • You could even choose the pro mode to let the app do all the heavy lifting for you.
Track progress and celebrate your wins!

Cookie Jars are your personalized financial goals, the cornerstone of effective budgeting. They help you plan smarter ways to save while tracking how your income drives growth. Each Cookie represents a small milestone, achieved month by month, that fills your Cookie Jar and brings you closer to your big goals. Together, they make saving simple and rewarding!
